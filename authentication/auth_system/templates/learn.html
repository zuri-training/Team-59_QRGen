QR Codes Solution

Meetings

QR codes are a great way to get people to join your various meeting using Zoom, Google Meet, Slack etc. They are easy to create and share, and they are ready to be used immediately.

When you create a QR code for your meeting, it will appear on the screen and can be scanned by anyone with a mobile device that has the appropriate app installed.

Attendance

Tracking attendance is so easy with QR codes! No more writing down attendance sheets, or clocking in. Just use attendance tracking QR codes to let everyone know who was there and who wasn't. It's great for tracking people at school and at work.

You can use it on an employee ID badge, or on an access key card to get into your office or building.

Business

Business QR codes are an easy way for you to connect with potential customers by giving them access to information about your products and services right from their phones. QR codes make it possible for people who don't know what they're looking at to find out more about what you offer.

All you have to do is create your business QR code and send it to the world! A single scan can turn to people connecting with you through social media, subscribing to your email newsletter, or a new customer!.

Email

Email message QR codes can make life easier for your customers, clients, and the rest of your world. With this QR code, customers can skip the whole "call or text me" step and just send your business a quick email.

When people scan it with their phone, they'll be directed straight to their email app with an email draft already typed in. They can add additional details if they want. And then just press send. It's that easy!
